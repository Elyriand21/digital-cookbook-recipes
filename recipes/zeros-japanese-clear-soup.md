## Ingredients
- 4 cups beef broth
- 4 cups water
- 5 cups chicken stock
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 1 carrot, large
- 1/2 onion
- 6 cloves garlic
- 2 tbsp Hondashi
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp soy sauce

## Instructions
1. Slice carrot into smaller pieces. Chop 1/2 onion into quarters. Chop 6 cloves of garlic. Saute all of these until fragrant.
2. Combine liquids, seasonings, hondashi, and add in the sauted vegetables into a large pot.
3. Simmer for 2 hours on low heat.
4. Once 2 hours has passed, strain out all solids.
5. Simmer for another 2 hours on low heat. Spoon into bowl and enjoy!
