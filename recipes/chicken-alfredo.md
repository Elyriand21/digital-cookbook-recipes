## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts
- 200g fettuccine
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 cloves garlic
- Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Boil pasta.
2. Sauté chicken.
3. Add garlic and cream.
4. Combine and serve.
