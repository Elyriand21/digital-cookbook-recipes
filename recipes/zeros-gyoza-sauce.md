## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rice vinegar
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/3 cup green onions
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/2 tsp minced garlic
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1/2 tbsp sake
- 1 tsp sugar

## Instructions
1. Place all of the ingredients into a small pot and heat on low until sugar is dissolved.
