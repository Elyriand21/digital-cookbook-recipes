## Ingredients
- large flour style tortillas (I like Mission GF Tortillas)
- shredded cooked chicken
- buffalo wing sauce (such as Frank’s or Sweet Baby Ray’s)
- chopped green onions
- shredded Mexican cheese blend
- ranch dressing, for dipping

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken with chopped green onions and buffalo wing sauce to taste, stirring until evenly coated. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
2. Heat a flat skillet or griddle over medium heat and spray with nonstick spray. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
3. Place a tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle a layer of shredded cheese on the bottom. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
4. Add some of the buffalo chicken mixture on top of the cheese, then sprinkle a bit more cheese on top. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
5. Place another tortilla on top, press gently, and cook until the bottom tortilla turns golden brown. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
6. Flip the quesadilla and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese has melted. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
7. Remove the quesadilla from the skillet and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing into wedges. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
8. Serve warm with ranch dressing on the side for dipping. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

## Notes
- You can use rotisserie chicken or cooked chicken breasts shredded for convenience. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
- Adjust the amount of buffalo sauce to suit your spice preference. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
- Serve with ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
