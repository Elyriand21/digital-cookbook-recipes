## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup chicken stock or broth
- 1/2 cup Tamari (or your choice of soy sauce)
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1/4-1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients and stir
2. If marinade does not cover meat, add 1/4 cup of stock/broth and soy sauce.
