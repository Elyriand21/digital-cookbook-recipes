## Ingredients
- 3 medium-size chicken tenderloins
- 9 medium asparagus spears
- 5 eggs
- 3 red thai chiles
- 5 cloves garlic
- 3/4 parsley branches
- 3 cups breadcrumbs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 3/4 tsp garlic powder
- 3/4 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp togarashi
- 8 tbsp butter, unsalted
- 3 tbsp sweet chili crisp
- 1/2 white onion, halved
- 1/2 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Soak chicken in milk.
2. Make slit length-wise in chicken, season w/ salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and togarashi. Rub into chicken.
3. Dip seasoned chicken in egg and breadcrumbs. Repeat once.
4. Put asparagus into aluminium foil. Add generous amount of butter and a bit of oil. season with salt and pepper. Dice 3 thai chiles and spread inside foil. Add 5 cloves of garlic. Put both 1/4 onions in foil. Wrap & put in oven for 13-15 minutes.
5. Heat pan on stove top with butter. Fry chicken until golden brown. Remove chicken and place in oven until 165°F (74°C) internal temp.
6. Fry eggs in same butter, adding salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, parsley/cilantro flakes.
7. Plate chicken and then place asparagus and egg on opposite sides of the bowl. Take hot butter and spoon over chicken and asparagus.
8. Add sweet chili crisp on egg, top with parsley flowers and enjoy!
