## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground chicken
- 3/4 cup rice
- 1/4-1/2 tbsp onion powder
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 1/2 tbsp hoison sauce
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2-3 sheets nori seaweed
- 1 tbsp neutral oil (avocado, etc)
- 4-5 rice paper sheets
- 1 avocado
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp parsley

## Instructions
1. Wash rice until water runs clear. Cook rice according to package instructions.
2. Mix chicken w/ onion powder, minced garlic, soy sauce, hoison sauce, and black pepper.
3. Shape chicken into musibi size (A good way to do that is with a spam container)
4. Add oil and cook chicken to 165°F (74°C) internal temp.
5. Cut nori sheets in half lengthwise. Add cooked rice to center of nori. (Season with furikake if desired)
6. Place cooked chicken on top of rice. Wet the end of the nori and wrap around rice and chicken to form musibi.
7. Soak rice paper sheets in cold water. Tightly wrap musibi in rice paper. Add cayenne pepper and parsley flakes on top of rice paper.
8. Fry each side of musibi in oil until golden brown (about 1-2 minutes)
9. Drizzle spicy mayo for garnish and enjoy!
