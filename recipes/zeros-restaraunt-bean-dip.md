## Ingredients
- 8oz La Banderita Refried Beans
- 2 tbsp & 2 tsp Lactose Free Milk (or regular milk)
- 1 tsp Red Hot hot sauce
- 1 tsp Valentinas hot sauce
- 1-2 cups 4 Cheese Mexican blend shredded cheese
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp parsley flakes
- Tortilla chips for dipping

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Combine all ingredients except cheese. Mix thoroughly.
3. Spoon half of mix into a 6in (15cm) baking dish. Top with half of cheese. Repeat with remaining bean mix and cheese.
4. Top with parsley flakes. Put in oven for 15 minutes.
